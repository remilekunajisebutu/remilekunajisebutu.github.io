About Me

I am a data analyst and technology consultant with over 10 years of combined experience in IT operations, security, business strategy, and analytics. Throughout my career, I have partnered with organizations across multiple industries, including healthcare, to optimize operations, strengthen security frameworks, enhance reporting capabilities, and support data driven decision making. My consulting work on healthcare focused initiatives involving operational analysis, system optimization, performance reporting, and compliance support strengthened my understanding of how data directly impacts patient outcomes, revenue cycle performance, and organizational efficiency.

With a Bachelor’s degree in Management and Computer Science and a Master’s in Management Information Systems, I bring a strong blend of technical expertise and strategic business insight. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into actionable intelligence through data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling. I am particularly passionate about healthcare analytics, where data can be leveraged to improve operational performance, financial outcomes, and overall patient experience.
