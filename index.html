About Me

I'm a passionate data analyst and consultant with over six years of experience in IT operations, security, and business strategy. Throughout my consulting career, I have worked with organizations to optimize operations, enhance security, and drive business growth. Over the years, I developed a deep appreciation for the power of data in making informed decisions, which led me to transition into the field of data analytics. With a background in both technology and management, I bring a strategic perspective to solving complex data challenges. From cleaning and analyzing complex datasets to building dynamic visualizations and predictive models, my goal is to turn data into actionable intelligence. I believe that data is more than numbers—it’s a powerful tool for shaping strategies and creating impactful business outcomes.
